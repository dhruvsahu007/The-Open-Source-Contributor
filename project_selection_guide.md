## ‚ö†Ô∏è Red Flags to Avoid

### Project Issues ‚ùå
- **No Recent Activity**: Last commit > 6 months ago
- **Poor Documentation**: Unclear setup or usage
- **Unresponsive Maintainers**: No response to issues/PRs
- **Complex Architecture**: Overly complicated for your skill level

### Issue Problems ‚ùå
- **Unclear Requirements**: Vague or ambiguous descriptions
- **Scope Creep**: Issues that keep expanding
- **Conflicting Solutions**: Multiple people working on same issue
- **Missing Context**: No clear problem statement

### Community Issues ‚ùå
- **Toxic Environment**: Unwelcoming or hostile community
- **Poor Communication**: No clear channels for help
- **Inconsistent Standards**: Unclear contribution guidelines
- **No Mentorship**: No support for new contributors
